Gettin' Jiggy With Jekyll

Sep 17th, 2012

I’m really into making terrible blog post titles. Luckily, I don’t care if you feel anything but amused.

I’m from a Drupal/Wordpress background, so when I was asked to create a site that would preferably not require maintenance of a server and was presented with Jekyll, I was excited, but underestimated my database addiction, by a lot.

I created this blog as a venting of my frustrations and a written expression of the bizarre happy dance I do when I finally get things right. Overall I would describe my experience as both perplexing and satisfying, the yin and yang of what MAW would call LEARNIN’. Chances are, this blog will pose more questions than answers, so I can’t understand why anyone would want to read this, but who knows. Maybe if only to satisfy the frustrations of other n00bs looking for comfort, ie “dude, I totally get you” or “dude, you’re fucking crazy.”

Ta-da.
